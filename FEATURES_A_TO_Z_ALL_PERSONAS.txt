A) Platform / Core (shared for everyone)
1) Authentication & Accounts
- Sign up / sign in
- Role-based access: Job Seeker, Recruiter, College Admin/Placement Cell, Super Admin
- Password reset / email verification (optional)

2) Role-Based Dashboards
- Dedicated dashboards per role (job seeker, recruiter, college admin)
- Personalized navigation + quick actions

3) Notifications & Communication
- In-app notifications
- Email notifications (assignment, schedule, reminders)
- Status update notifications (application stage updates)

4) Real-Time Status Tracking (Transparency Layer)
- Stage-wise pipeline status: Applied → Shortlisted → Test → Interview → Offer/Rejected
- Timeline/audit trail for actions taken
- Candidate-visible status + recruiter-visible pipeline

5) File & Document Management
- Resume upload
- Portfolio attachments (optional)
- Exported resume storage (optional)

6) Search, Filters, Sorting
- Candidate search (recruiter/admin)
- Job search (job seeker)
- Filters by skills, experience, location, score, test performance

7) Analytics & Reports (Core)
- Funnel analytics (applications → shortlist → interview → offer)
- Outcome tracking (callback rate, placement rate)
- Export reports (CSV/PDF) (optional)

8) Security & Compliance (Core)
- Data privacy controls
- Access logs (optional)
- Rate limiting / abuse protection (optional)
